Ip Man (2008)

2012-08-27

Great martial arts choreography, some of the best I have seen; two gorgeous guys, one the arrogant rural martial arts expert, and another, the Janasese military leader, who is among the prettiest guys I've seen ever.

There is nothing really new here, and the lead actor could be more interesting. Sadly, each of these oriental martial arts actors have Jet Li to match against, and that's a high standard of appeal. I am still giving this one high marks because I seriously enjoyed it, me being a sucker for its brand of formula.
