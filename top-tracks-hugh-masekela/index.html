top tracks: Hugh Masekela

2014-03-15

Although he made a lot of trash, Hugh Masekela remains one of the great South African artists. After all we judge artists based on their best works right? We are nice folk.

2 masterpieces

  • Ha Lese Le Di Khanna (Still Grazing - 2004)
  • Stimela (Hope - 1993)

3 excellent works

  • Grazing In the Grass (Still Grazing - 2004)
  • Bajabula Bonke (Still Grazing - 2004)
  • Yei Baa Gbe Wolo (Introducing Hedzoleh Soundz - 1973)

There are separate posts where I celebrate his two albums, Introducing Hedzoleh Soundz and Still Grazing.
