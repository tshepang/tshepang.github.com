discarding Scribes for Geany

2009-01-23

The version of Scribes in development didn't prove too satisfactory after a while (black background which I didn't care to investigate how to revert to normal, among whatever other trouble I don't remember) and there was me also unloving the idea of switching text editors, so decided to get stuck with Geany, and am not complaining much. It's been a year-and-a-half of pleasure and pain and it was well worth it.
