The American (2010)

2011-04-28

A very fresh assassin movie with great romance (did you see that gal?) and sound effects. The story is so thin (and rather meditative) that I don't know what to make of the movie. Many unexplained things happen, but I guess that's not exactly a weakness. Rather, it's implied. The uncomfortable situation our protagonist finds self in is quite edgy, and it makes me wanna NOT become a gangster. The intense violence is always unsettling, especially if it's merely imminent.
