'Toxic' by Britney Spears

2011-01-14

I had developed a negative attitude towards Britney Spear's music due bad quality (though to be fair, I haven't listened to much of her work, all but the most popular few tracks). But there is one particular gem, Toxic, and I was surprised to find that it's critically acclaimed, especially since I reduced it to a guilty pleasure.
